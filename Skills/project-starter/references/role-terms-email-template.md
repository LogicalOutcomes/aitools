This email does not replace any existing contractor or employment agreement — confirm terms with your organization's legal or HR contact before use.

If we need to change the scope of your work, it must be agreed to in writing by both parties (e-mail is fine).

Please refer to the overall project work plan and team contact information at any time. Should there be a change to the Project Charter, you will be notified. We will not make any change to the scope or budget of your work without discussing with you first.

If you have questions, comments or suggestions, please let us know.

[Your Name], Project Manager
